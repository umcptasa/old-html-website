Survive CP

  • October 29th, 2017

  • 3:00pm-6:00pm

  • Nyumburu Cultural Center

  • Survive CP

Welcome, come one come all to Survive CP 2017, hosted by the Taiwanese American Student Association and the Filipino Cultural Association!!!!

The rules are simple. You will sign up as a team of two or we can assign you to another solo player. We will be combining these two man groups into a super group of 4 so that you may be better prepared for the challenges ahead. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, will be to navigate through the campus to each roadblock and complete the task at the location. After you and your team complete the tasks, you will be able to proceed to the finish line and win eternal glory (valid for up to 1 year).

However, it’s not as easy as you think: there will also be zombies looking to tag you out of the game everywhere on the map. You will have 2 lives when you embark on your journey, however your spare life will expire after you complete a certain number of challenges so be warned. If you get tagged you will join the zombie team. Weaponry not included.

TLDR: Reach every checkpoint while avoiding zombies.

This is a fun way to get out of your dorm and compete with your friends or just have a good time! We can’t wait to see you!!!

Good Luck & Have Fun!!!!!

Sign-up deadline 10/25, Teams Finalized and notified by 10/28
