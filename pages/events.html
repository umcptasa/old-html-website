Survive CP

  • October 23, 2016

  • 4:00pm - 6:00pm

  • Nyumburu Amphitheater

  • Survive CP

GET HYPED because your friends at the Taiwanese American Student Association and the Filipino Cultural Association are holding our annual SurviveCP race! SurviveCP is our own spinoff of SurviveDC. Every year we invade campus and play a massive-multiplayer, zombie-based tagging game.

~~~SIGN UP~~~
***Deadline to signup is October 21st***
FREE SIGN UP HERE: www.ter.ps/survivecp
Sign up to volunteer here: www.ter.ps/volunteerz
Tell your kids, tell your wife. Let’s do this!

~~~RULES~~~
The rules are simple. You will sign up as a team of three or we can assign you to one. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, will be to navigate through the campus to each roadblock and complete the task at the location. After you and your team complete the tasks, you will be able to proceed to the finish line and take home gold!
However, it’s not as easy as you think: there will also be zombies looking to tag you out of the game everywhere on the map. After a 5 minute immunity period from start, the zombies have free reign. If you get tagged you will join the zombie team. Weaponry not included.
TLDR: Reach every checkpoint while avoiding zombies.
This is a fun way to get out of your dorm and compete with your friends or just have a good time! We can’t wait to see you!!!
Godspeed my comrades.

Contact Elton H Tian or Gabby Enguillado if you have any questions :)

Intercollegiate TASA East Coast Conference

  • February 16, 2017 -
    February 18, 2017

  • N/A

  • New York University

  • ITASA ECC 2017

Intercollegiate Taiwanese American Students Association (ITASA) invites you to be part of our journey. The three-day event will take place on Feb 16 to Feb 18 at New York University’s main campus located in the thriving heart of lower Manhattan.

This coming conference is titled "Intertrak". Intertrak is a train that is speeding through a series of workshops covering cultural, personal and career developments. Intertrak invites passengers who identify with or are interested in Taiwanese American and Asian American culture. Boarding on Intertrak, we want passengers to connect with each other on a deeper level and build lifelong networks with like-minded individuals.

Are you ready to share a ride with people who might become your lifelong friends?!

If you have any questions please reach out to itasa2017ecc@gmail.com

More details to come soon!
